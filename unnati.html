UNNATI

Unnati is a social club. Unnati means Progress, this club will help the students for the progress of there different skills.
To assist students with like interests and talents to become better acquainted.
To afford students the opportunity to carry classroom experiences over into the cocurricular life of the campus.
To give students a chance to develop social attributes.
To permit students a chance to participate in the democratic process.
To develop leadership qualities among students.
To assist in deepening students sensitivity.
To assist in the development of critical thinking skills.
